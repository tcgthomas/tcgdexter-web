# Profile your first deck

Time to use Dexter for the first time. We're going to paste a real, current-Standard deck list and see what comes back.

## Step 1 — go to the home page

Head to [the home page](/). The home page is the deck analyzer — there's a big text box waiting for a deck list.

## Step 2 — paste this deck list

Try this one. It's a stripped-down version of a beginner-friendly Charizard ex build:

```
Pokémon: 9
3 Charmander PAF 7
1 Charmeleon OBF 27
3 Charizard ex OBF 125
1 Pidgey MEW 16
1 Pidgeot ex OBF 164

Trainer: 39
4 Arven OBF 186
4 Iono PAL 185
2 Boss's Orders PAL 172
4 Rare Candy SVI 191
4 Ultra Ball SVI 196
4 Buddy-Buddy Poffin TEF 144
3 Nest Ball SVI 181
3 Earthen Vessel PAR 163
2 Super Rod PAL 188
3 Counter Catcher PAR 160
2 Lost Vacuum LOR 162
2 Bravery Charm PAL 173
2 Forest Seal Stone SIT 156

Energy: 12
12 Fire Energy SVE 2
```

Paste it in and hit **Analyze**.

## Step 3 — read what came back

Dexter will show you several modules. Here's what each one is telling you:

- **Overview Matrix** — the type and energy spread of the deck at a glance
- **Deck Sections** — how many Pokémon, Trainers, and Energy cards, and how those Trainers split between Supporter / Item / Tool / Stadium
- **Standard Legality** — is anything about to rotate out of Standard? You want this to say "ready."
- **Deck Score** — an A–F grade, broken down into rotation, consistency, evolution lines, and energy fit
- **Archetype Match** — does this deck look like a known meta archetype? You'll see "Charizard ex" come back with a high match %, since this is a classic build.
- **Price** — a live estimate from current shop listings
- **Card List** — the parsed deck list, so you can spot any errors in your paste

## What the score actually means

A score like "B+" isn't a verdict. It's a starting point. The component scores tell you *where* a deck is strong and weak — if you scored low on "energy fit," it might mean your energy count is wrong for your attackers; if you scored low on "evolution," your Stage 2 line might be missing Rare Candy support.
